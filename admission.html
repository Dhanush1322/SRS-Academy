How to Apply to SRS Academy?

We are excited that you are considering SRS Academy for your educational journey. To ensure a smooth admission process, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Fill out the Admission Form

Complete our online admission form with all necessary details.

Step 2: Submit Documents

Submit necessary documents, such as previous academic records and identification proof.

Step 3: Entrance Exam

Depending on the course you apply for, you may need to appear for an entrance exam to assess your basic understanding.

Step 4: Interview/Orientation

Shortlisted students will be called for an interview or orientation to discuss the course structure, faculty, and expectations.

Step 5: Payment and Enrollment

Upon successful admission, make the required fee payment to confirm your place in the program.

Our admissions team is available to assist you with any queries during the admission process.
