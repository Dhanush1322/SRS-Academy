Our Courses

At SRS Academy, we offer specialized coaching programs for a range of competitive exams. Whether you are aspiring for a career in engineering, medical sciences, or want to excel in pre-university exams, our carefully curated courses cater to diverse educational needs.

NEET Coaching

Prepare for NEET with expert faculty and a well-structured curriculum that covers all important topics. Our program helps students develop in-depth knowledge, problem-solving skills, and confidence to excel in the NEET exam.

JEE Mains Coaching

Our JEE Mains coaching program is designed to help students master Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. With rigorous training, detailed study material, and mock tests, we ensure our students are well-prepared for the exam.

JEE Advanced Coaching

Take your preparation to the next level with our JEE Advanced coaching. Focused on high-level concepts and problem-solving techniques, our program gives students the tools they need to tackle the most challenging problems in JEE Advanced.

CET Coaching

Our CET program prepares students to perform exceptionally well in the Karnataka Common Entrance Test. With a combination of classroom learning, self-study resources, and doubt-solving sessions, we ensure that students excel in this crucial exam.

PUC Science Coaching

Regular academic coaching for PUC Science students, ensuring their syllabus is well-covered alongside competitive exam preparation.

PUC Commerce Coaching

Quality coaching for PUC Commerce students, ensuring strong academic performance and understanding of key concepts.

✅ Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility for admission varies based on the course chosen. Below are the general requirements:

  • NEET / JEE Coaching:
    • Completion of 10+2 or equivalent exams in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics.
    • Minimum qualifying marks as prescribed by the relevant examination authorities.
  • CET Coaching:
    • Completion of 12th grade or equivalent exam with the required subjects.
    • Must be a resident of Karnataka for certain state-level programs.
  • Olympiad / NTSE / JSTS Coaching:
    • Open to students in secondary school (Class 9-12).
    • Designed for students who aim to compete at the national level.
  • Regular Academic Coaching:
    • Open to all students at the +2 level.

📝 Application Process

Follow these steps to enroll in your desired program at SRS Academy.

  1. Fill Out the Admission Form - Complete the online or offline form with your details.
  2. Submit Required Documents - Provide scanned or physical copies of your academic records and ID proof.
  3. Entrance Exam (If Applicable) - Required for courses like NEET, JEE, and Olympiads.
  4. Interview/Consultation (If Applicable) - Discuss course structure and expectations with our team.
  5. Fee Payment and Confirmation - Secure your spot by completing the fee payment.

🎯 Need assistance? Contact our admissions team for guidance.
